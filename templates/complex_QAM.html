Complex QAM Modulation and Demodulation

Quadrature Amplitude Modulation (QAM) combines amplitude and phase modulation by transmitting two signals (in-phase and quadrature) on the same carrier. Complex QAM is widely used in Wi-Fi, LTE, and digital TV.
